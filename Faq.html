Economy Questions

How will Endorblast make money if its not Pay To Win?

We that are working on Endorblast DON'T want to make a game where you will be able to pay your way to the top in form of in game items and buffs. Well it will have a store but not for that reason. Well other MMORPGs have no pay to win but in other form cosmetic items and that will our store be for, Cosmetic Items only.

In the store you will be able to buy clothes, pets and other cosmetic items that will NEVER effect your gameplay.
If that system for cosmetic items is good we will of course add more content to that store like limited time items.

But we will never just focus on items for the store to make money because content in the game is more important to make the game fun to play.
